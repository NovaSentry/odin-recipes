Gluten free pancakes

Description

A quick and easy gluten free (GF) pancake recipe

Ingredients

  • 125g GF plain flour
  • 1 egg
  • 250ml milk
  • butter

Method

  1. Put the flour in a bowl and make a well in the centre
  2. Crack the egg in the well and pour in a quarter of the milk
  3. Use an electric balloon whisk to thoroughly combine the mixture
  4. Once you have a paste, mix in another quarter and once lump free, mix in the remaining milk
  5. Leave to rest for 20 mins. Stir again before using
  6. Heat a small non-stick frying pan with a knob of butter. When the butter starts to foam, pour a small amount of the mixture into the pan and swirl around to coat the base â€“ you want a thin layer
  7. Cook for a few mins until golden brown on the bottom, then turn over and cook until golden on the the other side
  8. Repeat until you have used all the mixture, stirring the mixture between pancakes and adding more butter for frying as necessary
  9. Serve with agave syrup and a squeeze or orange juice or a pancake filling of choice
